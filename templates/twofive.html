7 Days strict Diet for 25 years old or below

Day 1

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast.

Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed vegetables.

Dinner: Baked salmon, brown rice, and roasted vegetables.

Snacks: Greek yogurt with berries, raw almonds.

Day 2

Breakfast: Oatmeal with sliced strawberries and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Lunch: Turkey and avocado whole grain wrap with a side salad.

Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables served with brown rice.

Snacks: Carrot sticks with hummus, unsalted mixed nuts.

Day 3

Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk and a banana.

Lunch: Lentil soup with a side of mixed greens and vinaigrette.

Dinner: Grilled shrimp with quinoa and roasted vegetables.

Snacks: Cottage cheese with pineapple, cucumber slices.

Day 4

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola and mixed berries.

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with assorted vegetables and a light vinaigrette.

Dinner: Baked cod with sweet potato and steamed broccoli.

Snacks: Apple slices with peanut butter, unsalted almonds.

Day 5

Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs.

Lunch: Whole wheat pasta with marinara sauce and a side salad.

Dinner: Grilled turkey burger with a whole grain bun, lettuce, and tomato, served with a side of roasted vegetables.

Snacks: Celery sticks with cream cheese, mixed nuts.

Day 6

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, low-fat yogurt, and chia seeds.

Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette.

Dinner: Baked chicken breast with quinoa and steamed vegetables.

Snacks: Cottage cheese with sliced peaches, raw almonds.

Day 7

Breakfast: Whole grain waffles with sugar-free syrup and fresh berries.

Lunch: Grilled vegetable and hummus wrap with a side salad.

Dinner: Baked salmon, brown rice, and stir-fried vegetables.

Snacks: Carrot sticks with guacamole, unsalted mixed nuts.
