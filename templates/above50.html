7 Days strict Diet plan for 50 years and above

Day 1

Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk and a sliced banana.

Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa, steamed vegetables, and a green salad.

Dinner: Baked fish with brown rice, roasted sweet potatoes, and broccoli.

Snacks: Greek yogurt with mixed berries, a handful of walnuts.

Day 2

Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs, a side of mixed fruit.

Lunch:Lentil soup with a side salad and a whole grain roll.

Dinner: Grilled shrimp with quinoa, stir-fried vegetables, and a green salad.

Snacks: Carrot sticks with hummus, a small apple.

Day 3

Breakfast:Oatmeal with chopped nuts, sliced strawberries, and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Lunch:Turkey and vegetable whole grain wrap with a side salad.

Dinner: Baked chicken breast with sweet potato, steamed asparagus, and mixed greens.

Snacks: Cottage cheese with pineapple, cucumber slices.

Day 4

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola, mixed berries, and a drizzle of honey.

Lunch: Grilled vegetable and hummus wrap with a side salad.

Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and a green salad.

Snacks: Apple slices with peanut butter, unsalted almonds.

Day 5

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast, a side of mixed fruit.

Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and vinaigrette.

Dinner: Grilled turkey burger with a whole grain bun, lettuce, and tomato, served with a side of roasted vegetables.

Snacks: Celery sticks with cream cheese, mixed nuts.

Day 6

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, low-fat yogurt, and chia seeds.

Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with assorted vegetables, feta cheese, and a light vinaigrette.

Dinner: Baked cod with quinoa, steamed broccoli, and a green salad.

Snacks: Cottage cheese with sliced peaches, raw almonds.

Day 7

Breakfast: Whole grain waffles with sugar-free syrup and fresh berries.

Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, diced vegetables, and a lemon-tahini dressing.

Dinner: Baked chicken breast with brown rice, stir-fried vegetables, and a green salad.

Snacks: Carrot sticks with guacamole, mixed nuts.
